About This Tool

GlimpSky Thread Viewer loads one public Bluesky post and reconstructs the widest publicly reachable conversation around it.

If you are annoyed that a whole conversation or argument becomes unreadable even when you are not the blocked person, this is the practical problem this viewer is built for.

It is built for threads that break in normal viewers because blocked or unavailable placeholders interrupt the public thread response.

All data comes from public Bluesky and AT Protocol endpoints. The viewer can surface thread breaks and sometimes recover still-public posts, but it cannot prove who blocked whom from public data alone.

Guides: Bluesky Thread Viewer, Bluesky Profile Viewer, Bluesky Followers Search.
