Bluesky Likes Viewer

Analyze what public Bluesky accounts like, when they like it, and how those patterns evolve over time.

Open Likes View Try Example Account

What this view helps you do

  • Understand topic preferences of a public account.
  • Search likes by keyword or regex.
  • Filter by date range to compare periods.
  • Inspect frequently liked authors.

How to inspect likes in 3 steps

  1. Open GlimpSky and enter a handle or DID.
  2. Select View Likes.
  3. Use filters for date, text, and author.

For very large accounts, initial counts can be partial. Clicking the likes count triggers a full recount.

Important limitation

Some liked records may be unavailable if they were deleted, deactivated, or temporarily unreachable through public endpoints.
